About us

Tatvamasi, London, Ontario, Canada, brings together Hindus and their cultural and religious traditions from Kerala, the “God’s own country”, a tropical paradise located in Southern India. Tatvamasi, a Sanskrit phrase, translated as “thou art that” is a description of the relationship between the Individual and the Brahman, the absolute reality. Mentioned in Chandogya Upanishad, Tat Tvam Asi is considered a Mahavakya and is revealed by Uddalaka Aruni to his son Svetaketu. Similar conclusions have been arrived by many yogis and saints, an example being: Aham Brahmasmi “I am Brahman” as mentioned in Brihadarankya Upanishad.
